About

SpiceTraders is a project being developed by 6 Computer Science Students from the University of York for our Engineering1 module. We continued the development from where group 22 left off.

The project consists the game SpiceTraders, the website, Requirements deliverable, Architecture deliverable, Method selection and planning, Risk assessment deliverable, and mitigation and implementation deliverable.
